🎮 How to Play

Explore a procedural island of biomes, towns & a downtown city. Gather resources, fight Wumpus, build, drive, and trade gold at the 🏪 Marketplace.

Controls

WASD Move · Space Jump · Shift Sprint
L-Click Attack / mine / chop trees
R-Click Place the selected block / prop
1–9 Pick a hotbar slot
Q Equip gun · F Eat food
E Enter / exit a vehicle
R Open the shop (inside the 🏪 Marketplace)

Tips

🪵 Chop trees for wood · 🏥 stand in the Clinic to heal · 🪙 sell loot for gold, then buy gear · 🛏️ your inventory & gold save to your account.
